SafelyExit

Manage your data and privacy – delete specific data or your full account

Delete All Data (Keep Your Account)

If you'd like to remove all saved data but keep your SafelyExit account, follow these steps:

  1. Login to your account
  2. Click the icon in the top right corner (there will only be one icon to click)
  3. Select "Account Information"
  4. Scroll down to the bottom
  5. Select "Clear All Data" and type CLEAR to confirm this action

Delete Your Account

If you’d like to delete your SafelyExit account and all associated data, follow these steps:

  1. Login to your account
  2. Click the icon in the top right corner (there will only be one icon to click)
  3. Select "Account Information"
  4. Scroll down to the bottom
  5. Select "Delete Account" and type DELETE to confirm this action
