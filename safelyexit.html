What is SafelyExit?

SafelyExit is a mobile app designed to simulate an incoming phone call — giving you a believable way to exit awkward, unsafe, or escalating situations.

How It Works

  • 📱 Launch the app and set a delay timer
  • 📞 Your screen shows a realistic incoming call
  • 🚶‍♀️ Step away with a valid reason — no confrontation needed
